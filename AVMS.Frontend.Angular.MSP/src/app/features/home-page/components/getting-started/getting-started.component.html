Getting Started
KALIBR Cloud Confidence
Brings a simple approach to reducing your customer’s cloud allocation costs by enabling your teams to gain visibility of the current cloud resources, schedule or scale instances when they are idle to avoid incurring additional costs to the customer’s bill, and scaling up and down prod and 24x7 resources as per your customer's needs.

Cloud Account Setup
Onboarding your customer’s cloud account gives you visibility into current cloud resources and their recommendations so your team can start building schedules (on/off) to be applied based on your customer’s needs.
Onboard your first cloud account.
Custom Schedules
Schedules are used to automatically manage the state of the cloud resources (allocated - ON / deallocated - OFF) and therefore save money through scheduling instances. Additionally, you can build your schedule times (on/off) to keep your instances running at the right time and stopped when not being used.
